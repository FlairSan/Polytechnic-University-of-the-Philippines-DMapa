D. Mapa: PUP A. Mabini Campus



Pool

One of the sport facilities of PUP. It is an olympic-sized swimming pool that is capable of handling several swimming classes and student athlete activities simultaneously

Chapel

The Ecumenical Chapel also known as the Interfaith Chapel is a nondenominational Christian church and it is the only Religious Facility of PUP A. Mabini Campus. It is for students and faculty members of different faith

Oval

The Track and Field Oval also known as the Oval is a lawn and an atheletics track. It is one of the sport facilities of PUP

Main Building

The main academic building of PUP A. Mabini Campus

Charlie Building

A structure dedicated to the first victim of the Marcos Regime named Charlie Del Rosario. An activist and desaparacido, he advocated rights for the oppressed.

Lagoon

A mossy creek with trees which is a relaxing and waiting area for students while waiting for their next class. It is recently renovated

Canteen

A place that serves food for the students of PUP A. Mabini Campus.

Laboratory Highschool

One of the premier high school in Manila

About:

D. Mapa (Pronounced as "The Mapa") is a PUP map system created by the students of PUP. Hover your mouse over the names of the places to see the information surrounding the building of Polytechnic University of the Philippines A. Mabini Campus.
